Our Heritage

A Legacy Woven with Care

From a modest family workshop in 1975, we've spent decades perfecting the art of textile craftsmanship. What began with the intricate weaving of silk sarees has evolved into a legacy of creating exquisite bedding linens that bring comfort and elegance to hotels, hospitals, spas, and salons.

Our family's passion for detail, commitment to quality, and willingness to innovate have allowed us to adapt our craftsmanship to meet the needs of discerning establishments like yours. We take pride in marrying time-honored traditions with modern designs, ensuring that every piece we create reflects our dedication to excellence.

By choosing our linens, you're not just selecting a product; you're embracing a story of heritage, meticulous care, and forward-thinking design. We're excited to partner with you, bringing a touch of our family's warmth and expertise to your spaces.
